Recruiting Season Ready: Resumes and LinkedIn

Your resume and LinkedIn profile are crucial first impressions in today’s job market. This workshop will help you craft a standout resume and an effective LinkedIn profile. Learn to highlight accomplishments, use impactful language, and tailor your resume for specific roles. We'll also cover LinkedIn strategies to connect with recruiters and expand your network. Be sure to review our Resume & LinkedIn Checklist prior to this Career Lab!

Recruiting Season Ready: Github

A strong GitHub profile is one of the easiest ways to make sure you are able to show clear evidence for your coding skillset. This workshop will teach you how to present your coding projects and contributions effectively on GitHub. Learn best practices for organizing repositories, writing meaningful commit messages, and showcasing your coding skills. We'll also cover how to make your repositories recruiter-friendly. Whether new to GitHub or refining your profile, this session will prepare you to showcase your technical expertise. Be sure to review our GitHub Checklist prior to this Career Lab!
